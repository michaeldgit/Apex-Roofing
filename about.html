About Apex

Founded 2005. Denver-owned. Storm-tested.

We started Apex after the 2004 Cherry Creek hail storm cleaned out a generation of Front Range roofs — and a generation of Front Range homeowners got cleaned out by out-of-state storm chasers. We built the company we wish had knocked on our door.

Our Story

A different kind of roofing company

Apex Roofing was founded by Nate Voss and his brother Adrian in 2005 out of a pickup truck in Lakewood. Nineteen years later we've installed more than 9,400 roofs across the Front Range — and we've still never hired a subcontractor.

Every Apex installer is a full-time W-2 employee with benefits, training, and skin in the game. That's why our crews can finish a full tear-off in a single day and still leave your yard cleaner than they found it.

We're licensed in the State of Colorado (RC-24817), fully insured, and proud members of the Roofing Contractors Association of Colorado.
