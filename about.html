About us

Apex Roofing is Denver's #1 trusted residential and commercial roofing contractor, serving homeowners and businesses across the Front Range for more than twenty years. From a single missing shingle to a full commercial roof system, every project gets the same attention to detail.

Our approach is simple: show up when we say we will, tell you exactly what your roof needs — and what it doesn't — and stand behind the work with a transferable lifetime workmanship warranty. No subcontracted day labor, no high-pressure sales pitch, no surprise charges at the end.

Our Story

Apex Roofing started in 2005 with a small crew of local installers who were tired of watching Denver homeowners get pushed into roofs they didn't need. Three guys, one pickup truck, and a stack of storm-damage estimates in Highlands Ranch. We grew the old-fashioned way — one honest job and one referral at a time.

Two decades later, we're an Owens Corning Preferred Contractor, a GAF Certified installer, and a GoGreen Home Energy Financing service provider, with thousands of Front Range roofs behind us. The way we work hasn't changed: every estimate is walked personally, every job is run by a W-2 crew lead, and no one pays a dime until the final walk-through is signed.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver lasting value to every customer through expert craftsmanship, premium materials, and dependable service. From the first estimate to the final cleanup, we go the extra mile to ensure your roofing system is built to last and your expectations are not just met, but exceeded.

We understand that every property is personal—whether it's your home, your business, or your investment. That's why, when you hire us, your property becomes our responsibility—and we take that responsibility personally. Every project is handled with care, precision, and respect, because at the end of the day, your roof protects what matters most.
