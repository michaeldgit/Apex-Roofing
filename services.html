Roof Repair

A leak in the ceiling at home, a lifted shingle after a hailstorm, or a torn membrane over a warehouse floor. Whether it's a single-family house or a commercial building, a roof problem costs more the longer it waits. We mobilize fast on both, diagnose the actual source instead of patching the wet spot, and keep the weather out.

What's Included

  • Leak tracing on shingle, tile, metal, and single-ply membrane
  • Hail, wind, and storm damage repair
  • Flashing, parapet, chimney, and curb re-seals
  • Emergency tarping, 24/7

Roof Replacement

A residential replacement takes a few days. A commercial replacement takes longer and has to happen around tenants, deliveries, and daily operations. Either way, we strip the roof to the deck, fix whatever's hiding underneath, and rebuild with materials rated for Front Range hail, wind, and sun. Phased jobs stay weathertight every night, so the building keeps running.

What's Included

  • Residential: asphalt, tile, and standing-seam metal
  • Commercial: TPO, EPDM, PVC, modified-bitumen, and metal
  • Full tear-off with deck inspection and rot repair
  • Transferable lifetime workmanship warranty

Roof Inspection

Our inspection gives homeowners and property managers the same thing: a written report with photos, remaining service life, and what it would take to extend it.

What's Included

  • Residential: 50-point inspection, free for Denver metro homeowners
  • Commercial: walkthrough with infrared moisture survey and drone imagery
  • Photo-documented written report delivered within 24 hours
  • Formatted for adjusters, lenders, closings, and board packets

Roof Cleaning

Moss, algae, and black streaks make a house look tired. Leaves, debris, and clogged drains turn into ponding water on a commercial roof. Either way, buildup holds moisture and shortens the life of the system. Our low-pressure soft-wash and debris service lifts all of it off without blasting granules loose or damaging membranes.

What's Included

  • Residential: low-pressure soft-wash, moss and algae removal, gutter flush
  • Commercial: debris clearing, drain and scupper flushing, membrane wash
  • Safe on asphalt, tile, metal, TPO, EPDM, and PVC
  • Optional zinc treatment to slow regrowth
