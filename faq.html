Common Questions

Straight answers to the questions we get asked most often.

How fast can you respond to a leak?

For active leaks, we dispatch a tarp crew within 24 hours across the Denver metro, and most repairs are scheduled within 48 hours. Call (303) 481-2739 after hours and our on-call project manager will answer.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We carry a Class A general contractor license, $2M general liability coverage, and full workers' compensation on every crew. The license and certificate of insurance are sent with every proposal.

Do you handle insurance claims for hail and wind damage?

We work with every major carrier. Our team meets your adjuster on the roof, provides a line-item estimate at Xactimate pricing, and handles the supplemental process if the scope changes mid-project.

What's included in the free roof inspection?

A 50-point assessment of the exterior and attic, every finding photographed, and a written report delivered within 24 hours. It's free for Denver metro homeowners, and there's no obligation to book any work afterward.

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

Most residential replacements wrap in 1 to 3 days. Commercial projects are phased to keep the building weathertight and operational, with timelines scoped to the square footage and membrane system.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We partner with Hearth and GreenSky to offer introductory 0% rates and terms up to 15 years. Pre-qualification is a soft credit pull and takes about 60 seconds on your phone.

What warranty do you provide?

Every installation carries a lifetime workmanship warranty that transfers when you sell. Manufacturer warranties on materials range from 20 to 50 years depending on the system, with NDL options available for commercial work.

What areas around Denver do you serve?

The full Denver metro, from Boulder and Broomfield down to Castle Rock and Parker, and east to Aurora. We also handle commercial work statewide for portfolio clients.

Do you work on homes and commercial buildings?

Both. We run separate residential and commercial crews, each with project managers dedicated to their segment, so a single-family hail repair gets the same attention as a 100,000-square-foot TPO replacement.
