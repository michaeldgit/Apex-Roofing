Hail hit. Water in the attic. Roof older than your mortgage. Sound familiar? You're not alone. We work with Denver homeowners, HOAs and property managers solving these exact problems with roofing solutions that actually last.

Apex is a storm-rated roofing concern with an innate understanding of Colorado weather. We install Class 4 impact-rated systems across the Front Range, with specialisations in storm & hail claims, full replacements, targeted repairs, free 24-hour inspections and new construction.

Based in Denver with crews on Colorado Blvd, we're in Aurora, Lakewood, Highlands Ranch and Parker every other week working projects. Our teams are all W-2 and always available. We're unconditionally focused on one thing: your roof outlasting your mortgage.
