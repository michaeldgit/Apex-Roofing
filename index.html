How it works

From storm to new roof — in four moves.

Same rhythm every job. No surprises on day three, no change orders without a signature.

Climb the roof

Our estimator climbs the roof — not the driveway. Every hailstrike chalked, every plane measured, every penetration photographed. You get the full report that afternoon.

Day 1 · 45 min

Meet the adjuster

We walk the roof with your insurance adjuster and point out what their scope missed. Most Apex jobs pick up supplemental funds at this stage — properly documented, never inflated.

Day 5 – 10

Install week

Tear-off at 7am, dry-in by lunch, shingles going down by mid-afternoon. Magnet swept across your driveway every evening. Most residential jobs wrap in two to three days flat.

Day 14 – 17

Final walk & warranty

We walk the property with you, hand over the manufacturer and workmanship warranties in a folder, and file your register card the same day. Our cell number is yours for the next twenty years.
