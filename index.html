#1 Roofing Company in Denver

Apex Roofing is a residential and commercial roofing contractor with over 25 years of experience. Our expertise ranges from single-family homes to multi-tenant residential projects and commercial and industrial facilities.

We are an Owens Corning Preferred Contractor as well as a GAF Certified roofing contractor, and a GoGreen Home Energy Financing certified service provider. We are the preferred contractor for roofs in the Denver metro area.
