How It Works

From first call to final nail in 4 simple steps

No surprises. No hidden fees. Just a clear path from storm damage to a brand-new roof.

1

Free Inspection

We climb the roof (not the driveway). Chalk every hit, photograph every plane, send you the full report that afternoon.

2

Adjuster Meet

We meet your insurance adjuster on the roof and walk through the damage together. Most jobs gain supplemental funds at this stage.

3

Install in 2–3 Days

Tear-off at 7am, dry-in by lunch, shingles by afternoon. Driveway swept with a magnet every evening. Most residential jobs wrap in 48–72 hours.

4

Final Walk & Warranty

We walk the property with you, hand over manufacturer and workmanship warranties, and file your register card the same day.
