Verify Our License

Colorado does not maintain a statewide roofing-contractor license database. Roofing contractors are licensed at the city and county level. Apex maintains active licenses with the cities of Denver, Aurora, Lakewood, Centennial, Arvada, Thornton, and Westminster, as well as Arapahoe, Jefferson, and Douglas counties. Call (303) 481-2739 and we'll send you copies.
