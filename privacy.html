Privacy Policy

What we collect, how we use it, and how to get it deleted.

Last updated: January 2026

Apex Roofing LLC ("Apex," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you visit apexroofing.co or request a quote, and how we use that information.

1. What We Collect

When you submit a form on our site, we collect your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, and any details you provide about your roofing needs. When you browse apexroofing.co, our hosting provider records standard server logs (IP address, browser type, referring page) to keep the site running and secure.

2. How We Use It

  • To respond to your estimate or inspection request
  • To schedule and perform the services you engage us for
  • To send occasional follow-ups about your project (text or email)
  • To comply with legal or regulatory obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third-party marketers.

3. Who We Share It With

We share limited information only with parties necessary to complete your project: manufacturers for warranty registration, insurance carriers when you authorize a claim, and payment processors. All third parties are bound by data-protection obligations.

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses minimal first-party cookies for session handling. We may use privacy-respecting analytics (like Plausible or Fathom) to measure aggregate traffic. We do not deploy cross-site advertising trackers.

5. Text Messages

If you provide a phone number, you consent to receive transactional text messages from Apex about your scheduled inspection, project updates, or warranty follow-ups. Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Message and data rates may apply.

6. Data Retention

We keep customer project records for seven years to honor warranty claims and meet tax and record-keeping obligations. Leads that do not become customers are purged after 24 months.

7. Your Rights

You may request a copy of the information Apex holds about you, ask us to correct inaccuracies, or request deletion of your records (subject to legal retention obligations). Email hello@apexroofing.co with your request.

8. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their information.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

Questions, concerns, or requests? Email hello@apexroofing.co or call (303) 481-2739. Mail: Apex Roofing LLC, 2400 S Colorado Blvd, Denver, CO 80222.
